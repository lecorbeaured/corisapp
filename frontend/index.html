Why CORIS

Built for people who want to own their data.

Ledger-style tracking

Every bill occurrence is stored as an immutable record. No silent edits, no retroactive changes — just a clean, auditable history.

Paycheck planning

Set your pay schedule and CORIS assigns each bill to the right paycheck window. Know exactly what's due before each payday.

No bank linking

Your bank credentials stay with your bank. CORIS never asks for account access, API keys, or third-party integrations. Period.

How it works

Three steps to organized bills.

1

Create bill templates

Add your recurring bills — rent, utilities, subscriptions. Set the frequency, due day, and typical amount.

2

Set your pay schedule

Tell CORIS when you get paid — weekly, biweekly, or monthly. It creates planning windows around your paydays.

3

Review and mark paid

Each paycheck window shows which bills are due. Mark them paid as you go. Get email reminders for bills due today.
