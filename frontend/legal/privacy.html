Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What We Collect

CORIS collects your email address (for authentication and email reminders) and the bill data you manually enter (template names, amounts, due dates, payment records). We do not collect bank data, financial account numbers, or any information beyond what you explicitly enter.

What We Don't Do

We never link to your bank. We never sell your data. We never share your information with advertisers or third-party data brokers. We do not use tracking cookies beyond what is necessary for authentication.

Data Security

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. Sessions use HTTP-only cookies with CSRF protection. All production traffic is encrypted via HTTPS. Your data is stored in a secured database with access limited to application processes only.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting support. Upon deletion, all bill templates, occurrences, planning data, and reminder logs are permanently removed.
